Run 'N Gun

Run ‘n Gun is a first person shooter made primarily using the Unity Game Engine and the Photon Networking Plugin. The game is multiplayer and based off of Photon’s cloud server. Players can connect and play from anywhere with a wifi connection. In the game players join either the blue team, red team, or play as a solo renegade. In the game, players are equipped with a gun and can shoot and kill other players. On death, players are re spawned, allowing for endless hours of fun. The game can support a total of 20 players at a time and can be played on mac, pc, and linux based systems.
